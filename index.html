Sabhi doubts clear kar dete hain

Main beginner hun, kabhi gym nahi gaya. Mere liye hai?
Bilkul! Most successful users are beginners. We start from basics - teaching body movement first, then building strength slowly. No judgement, just consistency matters!
Kya equipment chahiye?
Kuch nahi! All exercises are bodyweight-based. Just need small space (6x6 feet works). If you have dumbbells/bands, great - but not required at all.
Kitna time lagega results mein?
Results vary per person. Generally, 2-3 weeks mein energy boost feel hoga. 2-3 months mein visible changes with consistency. Remember - sustainable results take time, and that's healthy!
Main bahut busy hun, daily 1 hour karna padega?
Nahi! Start with just 15 minutes daily. When you have time, do 30-45 min. We give flexible plans that fit your schedule. Consistency beats duration!
Vegetarian diet hai, kaam karega?
Haan bilkul! 70% users are vegetarian. We optimize Indian vegetarian diet - dal, paneer, dahi, sabzi, fruits. Regional preferences (South Indian, Gujarati, Bengali) bhi covered!
Health problems hain (BP, diabetes, PCOS), kar sakta hun?
First, doctor se consult karo! If doctor clears you, we'll provide condition-specific modifications. Many users safely exercise with health conditions. Your safety is our priority.
Regional language mein support milega?
Haan! Hindi, English, Hinglish available. Need Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Gujarati? Let us know - we'll arrange it for you!
Agar plan pasand nahi aaya?
