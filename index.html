AI aur human coach dono kaise kaam karte hain saath mein? +
AI aapki details analyze karke daily workout plans, meal timing, aur progress tracking karta hai - ye instant hota hai aur 24/7 available hai. Human coaches ye AI-generated plans review karte hain, aapke doubts solve karte hain, form corrections dete hain, aur motivation provide karte hain. AI speed deta hai, coach personal touch deta hai. Main complete beginner hoon. Kya yeh mere liye kaam karega? +
Bilkul! Hamare zyada tar members beginners hain. AI aapki current fitness level ko samajh ke basic exercises se shuru karti hai. Human coaches ensure karte hain ki aap proper form seekho aur safely progress karo. Koi judgement nahi, sirf consistent improvement!
Koi equipment chahiye kya? +
Nahi! AI saare exercises body weight use karke design karta hai. Bas aapko 6x6 feet space chahiye. Agar dumbbells ya resistance bands hain toh AI aapke plan mein unhe include kar sakti hai - par required nahi hain.
Kitni jaldi results dikhenge? +
Results har insaan ke liye alag hote hain. Generally, 2-3 hafte mein aap zyada energetic feel karoge. AI progress track karta hai aur body mein visible changes 2-3 mahine mein dikhte hain consistency ke saath. Yaad rakho - sustainable, healthy results time lete hain. Quick fixes kaam nahi karte.
Main bahut busy hoon. Kya 15 minutes sach mein kaam karega? +
Haan! AI efficient workouts design karta hai jo short time mein maximum impact deti hain. Consistency duration se better hai. Roz 15 minutes, hafte mein 2 baar 1 hour se better hai. 15 min se shuru karo, jab time mile toh AI automatically plan badha degi.
Main vegetarian hoon. Kya meal plan mere liye kaam karega? +
Definitely! AI ko Indian vegetarian diets pe train kiya gaya hai. Dal, paneer, dahi, sabzi, fruits - jo aap pehle se khate ho usko AI optimize karegi. Regional preferences (South Indian, Gujarati, Bengali, etc.) fully covered hain.
