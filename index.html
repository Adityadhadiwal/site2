FAQs

Common Questions

Everything you need to know

I'm a complete beginner. Will this work for me?
Absolutely! Most of our members are beginners. We start with basics and progress gradually. You'll learn proper form, build strength slowly, and avoid injuries. No judgement, just consistent progress.
Do I need any equipment?
No! All exercises use your body weight. You just need a 6x6 feet space. If you have dumbbells or resistance bands, great - but they're not required.
How quickly will I see results?
Results vary by person. Generally, you'll feel more energetic in 2-3 weeks. Visible body changes take 2-3 months with consistency. Remember - sustainable, healthy results take time. Quick fixes don't last.
I'm very busy. Will 15 minutes really work?
Yes! Consistency beats duration. 15 minutes daily is better than 1 hour twice a week. Start with 15 min, increase when you have time. We design efficient workouts that deliver results.
I'm vegetarian. Will the meal plan work?
Definitely! We specialize in Indian vegetarian diets. Dal, paneer, dahi, sabzi, fruits - we'll optimize what you already eat. Regional preferences (South Indian, Gujarati, Bengali, etc.) fully covered.
I have health issues (BP, diabetes, PCOS). Can I do this?
Please consult your doctor first! If cleared, we'll provide condition-specific modifications. Many members exercise safely with health conditions. Your safety is our top priority - never skip doctor consultation.
How do I get support? Is there a coach I can talk to?
Premium members get direct WhatsApp and email access to certified coaches. Ask questions anytime, get form feedback, share concerns. We're real humans who care about your progress!
What if I don't like it? Can I get a refund?
Yes! 30-day money-back guarantee. If Premium isn't for you, email us within 30 days for a full refund. No questions, no hassle. We want happy members, not trapped ones.
How is this different from YouTube workout videos?
YouTube is great but generic. We personalize for YOUR fitness level, goals, time availability, home space, and Indian diet. Plus you get progressive plans that adapt, direct coach support, and accountability. Not one-size-fits-all.
